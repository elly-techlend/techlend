Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how TechLend collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Company Data: Details provided when registering your company, such as name, address, and contact information.
  • User Data: Login credentials, role assignments, and activity logs.
  • Financial Data: Loan, savings, and transaction records specific to your company.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide and improve our loan management services.
  • Ensure secure backups and activity monitoring.
  • Notify administrators of important updates.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal or company data. Data may be shared only with:

  • Authorized administrators within your company.
  • Service providers (e.g., cloud storage, email services) strictly for operational purposes.
  • Law enforcement if required by applicable law.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your company data at any time, subject to applicable laws.

6. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated to company administrators.

7. Superuser Access

The superuser has system-wide oversight but does not interfere with company-level operations unless required for compliance.
